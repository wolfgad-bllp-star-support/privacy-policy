Privacy Policy for "Break Language Learning Proje"

Effective Date: July 19, 2025

1. What Data We Collect

Our application saves your user settings locally on your device. This includes:

  • The selected voice for playback
  • The set speech rate
  • The list of blocked voices
  • Other interface settings for your convenience

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data is used only to ensure the correct operation of the application and to save your preferences between usage sessions.

3. Third-Party Services

Currently, our application does not use third-party services (such as advertising or analytics) that would collect your data.

4. Data Security and Deletion

You can delete all saved data at any time by clearing the application's data in your Android device's system settings.
